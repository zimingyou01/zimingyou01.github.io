Bio

I am a researcher currently in a gap year, actively applying for Ph.D. positions and seeking research internships. I obtained my B.S. degree in Beihang University and my M.S. degree in Peking University. My research interests focus on Reinforcement Learning and LLM/VLM-based (Multi-)Agent Systems.

I was fortunate to intern at TsinghuaNLP, supervised by Prof. Zhiyuan Liu and collaborating closely with Weize Chen and Ran Li . My future research aims to build better agentic systems via reinforcement learning, exploring RL-based approaches to enhance autonomy, adaptability, and collaboration in AI agents.
